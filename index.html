About

How I think
about marketing.

Great marketing rarely happens in silos. I enjoy connecting the dots — bringing together brand, performance marketing, partnerships, operations, and customer experience so they work as one system instead of separate projects. Whether that means launching a campaign, leading a cross-functional initiative, or figuring out why performance shifted, I'm happiest solving complex problems alongside great people. I lean on data to guide the decisions, but I never lose sight of the story — or the people — behind the numbers.

My marketing leadership spans Urban Adventures Companies and the Recreational Boating & Fishing Foundation (RBFF) — across fitness, wellness, hospitality, lifestyle brands, and national nonprofit marketing.

Connected marketing is stronger marketing
